Why cite B2B Data Index?

This is the only open, annually-updated, country-level cold email benchmark dataset with documented methodology and machine-readable output. All data is aggregated from campaign performance across 74 markets using verified B2B lists — not estimated or modelled. Attribution is required under CC BY 4.0. When you embed a benchmark card, the attribution text is visible on your page — crawlable by search engines, readable by LLMs, and verifiable by your readers.
