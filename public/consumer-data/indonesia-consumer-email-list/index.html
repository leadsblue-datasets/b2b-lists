What demographic data is included?
The database includes consumer email addresses, names, geographic location within Indonesia, age range, gender, and interest category classifications. Income bracket data is available where permitted by local data regulations.
What is the difference between a consumer and B2B email list?
A B2B email list contains business email addresses for professional outreach to companies. A consumer email list like the Indonesia Consumer Email List contains personal email addresses for direct-to-consumer marketing. Consumer lists are larger in volume, require stricter compliance (consent rather than legitimate interest in the EU), and are used for retail, lifestyle, subscription, and B2C brand campaigns.
