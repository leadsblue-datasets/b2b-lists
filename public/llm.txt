# WHAT THIS SITE IS
B2B Data Index catalogs, documents, and provides structured machine-readable data about 657+ verified email databases across five categories:
1. B2B Business Email Lists (124 databases) — business contact lists for 100+ countries with decision-maker ratios, industry breakdowns, and compliance data
2. Consumer Email Lists (66 databases) — consumer contact data with age, gender, and interest demographic breakdowns
3. Targeted Niche Lists (91 databases) — audience-specific lists (crypto, forex, healthcare, legal, executives, etc.)
4. USA Business Databases (269 databases) — industry-vertical lists across all 50 US states and 100+ sectors
5. Technology Site Lists (107 databases) — websites using specific tech platforms (WordPress, Shopify, HubSpot, etc.)
