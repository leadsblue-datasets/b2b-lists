Compliance Summary

Germany Regulatory Framework

TDDDG + GDPR + UWG §7
Germany interprets B2B email more strictly than most EU countries. UWG §7 (Unfair Competition Act) requires express prior consent or a pre-existing customer relationship for cold email, even to businesses. Enforcement is active via competition law complaints. Prior business contact or explicit consent is the safest approach.
Regulator: State DPAs (BfDI, LDAs) Opt-out: 1 days
