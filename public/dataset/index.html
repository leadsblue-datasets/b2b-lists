Benchmark Dataset 2026

The open benchmark dataset for B2B cold email campaign performance. 74 countries, 14 industries, 4 decision-maker seniority levels. Aggregated from real campaign data across verified B2B lists. Machine-readable JSON, embeddable HTML cards, live API. Attribution required under CC BY 4.0.

Methodology

Aggregated from campaign data across 74 markets. Benchmarks reflect well-targeted campaigns to verified decision-maker lists unless noted. Ranges represent 10th-90th percentile performance. Data updated annually.

All ranges represent 10th-to-90th percentile performance across well-targeted campaigns run on verified decision-maker lists. Campaigns with bounce rates above 3% or poor targeting are excluded from aggregation. Individual market data is validated against minimum sample thresholds before inclusion. See full methodology →
