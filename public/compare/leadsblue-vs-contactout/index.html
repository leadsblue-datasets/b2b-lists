Our Take

Which Should You Choose?

Neither LeadsBlue nor ContactOut is objectively superior — they serve different use cases, buyer profiles, and budget tiers. The right choice depends on three factors: your target geography, your deal economics, and whether you need a subscription workflow or a one-time export.

Choose LeadsBlue if: Agencies, SDRs, and B2B teams targeting international markets with a clear ICP. Its one-time purchase model and 200+ countries coverage make it the right fit for teams where that combination of price structure and geographic reach matches their outbound motion.

Choose ContactOut if: Recruiters and SDRs doing manual LinkedIn-based research for individual contacts. Its monthly subscription model and focus on LinkedIn network global suits teams where those characteristics are the primary requirements.

The honest bottom line: If your primary market is the US and you need a workflow-integrated platform, LeadsBlue is likely stronger. If you need international coverage at a one-time price with no subscription, LeadsBlue wins on economics. If budget is the constraint, always start with the cheaper option to validate your ICP before committing to an enterprise contract.
