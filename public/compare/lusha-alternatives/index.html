Best Lusha Alternatives

Lusha is excellent for individual contact lookups via LinkedIn — but it is credit-limited, expensive at scale, and not built for bulk list building. Here is what to use depending on your actual workflow.

Best For

Individual SDRs doing LinkedIn-first prospecting and targeted contact research on specific accounts

Common Limitations

How to Choose the Right Alternative

The right Lusha alternative depends on your workflow, target markets, and budget model. Use this decision framework:

🎯

If you research contacts one by one

Lusha and similar credit-based tools are designed for manual, individual contact research. If your SDR workflow involves researching 10–30 contacts per day before outreach, a credit model is appropriate and cost-efficient.

📋

If you build lists in bulk

For campaigns where you need 500–10,000 contacts at once, bulk database purchases deliver better economics. The per-contact cost of credit-based tools at bulk volume is 3–8x higher than database purchases.

🌐

If you target outside the US and UK

Lusha data quality for non-anglophone markets is noticeably thinner. For Germany, France, Asia-Pacific, or MENA targeting, purpose-built international databases consistently outperform credit-based tools in both record count and data accuracy.
