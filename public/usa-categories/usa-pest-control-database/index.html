USA Pest Control — Market Notes

Decision-Maker Context

Owner-operators control all purchasing at independent pest control companies. At mid-size companies (10-50 technicians), the owner with input from office managers handles vendor decisions. Purchasing decisions are typically fast — pest control operators are accustomed to making operational decisions quickly.

Outreach Strategy

Pest control outreach works with route optimization, recurring service agreement management, and digital inspection reporting framing. ServiceTitan, WorkWave, and PestPac dominate pest control software. Call recording and lead management tools are high-adoption categories — pest control companies generate a high volume of inbound calls from residential customers.

Key Market Facts

  • ▸ 27,000 pest control companies employing 160,000 workers in the US
  • ▸ US pest control market revenue exceeds $22 billion annually
  • ▸ Industry grows 5-7% annually driven by bed bug, termite, and mosquito demand
  • ▸ Recurring service agreements generate 65% of total pest control company revenue
