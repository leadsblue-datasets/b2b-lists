Frequently Asked Questions #

What's a good cold email open rate for Founder / Co-Founders?

22–32% is the standard range, with 27% median. Founders sit at the top because they manage their own inboxes and triage personally. SMB owners have the highest reply rates and shortest decision cycles. They can say yes immediately. Best for offers with clear, fast ROI.

How long should a Founder cold email sequence be?

4 emails over Day 1, Day 4, Day 9, Day 16. Shorter is better — these recipients see longer sequences as harassment.

What copy length works for Founders?

Short (60-100 words). Direct, ROI-focused.. Under-100-word emails outperform longer ones consistently. The CEO/Founder reads on mobile in 3 seconds — single-screen visibility is the operating constraint.

What subject lines work for Founder email?

Revenue growth or time-saving specific to their business type. Numbers in subject lines work for founders ("$2M ARR question") more than for any other tier. Generic "quick question" or "thoughts?" subjects burn first-impression credibility.

How does Founder reply rate compare to other roles?

Founders reply at 3–8% (median 5.5%) vs 2.5–6% for managers. Owner/Founder reply rates are highest, with shortest decision cycles. Best for fast-ROI offerings.
